About

Greetings,

Whether recording audio, designing and manipulation sound effects, or integrating audio into a game engine, using sound to help transform imagined concepts into an immersive reality is one of my favorite things. The art of sound, combined with the gracefulness of today's technology, is a powerful tool that can be used to build the kind of story that you want to tell. I would love to be a part of telling that story, and And I would love to help make this a reality for your project. With a high respect for the artistry and craftsmanship that goes into making video games and film, I do my best to breath life into each project via audio. I love a good story in both the film and game mediums. Let me be a part of telling your story through the art of sound.

You can email me at kimikomu@gmail.com.
