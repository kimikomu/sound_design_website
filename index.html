About

Sound Design and Audio Implementation

For Film and Video Games

Whether recording audio, designing and manipulation sound effects, or integrating audio into a game engine, using sound to help transform imagined concepts into an immersive reality is one of my favorite things. The art of sound, combined with the gracefulness of today's technology, is a powerful tool that can be used to build the kind of story that you want to tell. I would love to be a part of telling that story.

This year I am expanding my knowlege into the world of programmming and web development. My current programming language knowledge includes HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript. This site is one of my projects. You can veiw more programming projects on my GitHub page.

Audio Services

Asset Creation and Editing

One of my favorite parts of the process! My goel in asset creation is to design interesting sound effects that let your unique vision shine through.

Audio Implementation

How audio is implemented into a video game is just as important as good asset creation. I will make sure your game is using audio the way it needs to be for maximum effect.

Restoration and Clean-up

Sometimes audio files need a little TLC. I am meticulous when it comes to removing unwanted noise from pre-recorded audio to get your project sounding as clean as possible.

Mixing and Mastering

When all elements are complete and in the right place, it's time to bring everything together into a cohesive whole. I make sure all audio assets and music are stacked correctly for a complete and balanced sound design.

Music Editing and Composition

Games and film need different things when it comes to music. Whether you need a soundtrack for your film, or stems created for your interactive score, I taylor music to the needs of your project.

More

Voice recording, Foley, field recording, MIDI sequencing and more. Whether a video game, a web game, or a film, let me know the audio needs of your specific project to help your story sound amazing.
